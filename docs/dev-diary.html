Reflection

What worked best for me during CCL2 was the design phase. Having full creative freedom made me feel deeply engaged with the project. Seeing the design and prototype come together was the first major success moment.

During development, almost every working feature felt like a small success. Especially after a private break, coming back and pushing through made the progress feel even more meaningful. Hosting was a struggle, but finishing the project and standing behind it made me genuinely proud.
