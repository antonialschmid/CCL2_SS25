Project Summary

Dear Body is a poetic, blog-based web application where users can write anonymous letters to their body or specific body parts.

The project creates a soft and intimate digital space for reflection, emotional release, and self-address — inspired by zines, diaries, and feminist publishing.

All letters are published anonymously. The absence of public identity shifts the focus away from performance and towards care, vulnerability, and personal expression.

Key Features

  • Public feed of anonymous letters
  • Login & registration for writing and resonating
  • Writing letters addressed to the body or body parts
  • User profile with personal letters
